Inside this pack of 74 hand-drawn images you will find a variety of fantasy items, including daggers, swords, axes, staves, shields, scrolls, spellbooks, potions, torches, war banners, tools, and more! Perfect for an RPG adventure, a dungeon-crawling card game, a medieval trick-taking game, a rogue-like video game and many other projects!

Each image is at least 825 x 1125 resolution, in PNG format with a transparent background.
